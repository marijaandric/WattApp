Green energy

Renewable energy comes from sources that are constantly and naturally renewed (hence the name), such as wind power and solar energy. Renewable energy is also often called sustainable energy. Renewable energy sources are the opposite of fossil fuels, like coal and gas, which are a finite energy source..

Why You Should Use Solar Power

Solar power is a great way to reduce your carbon footprint. There's nothing about solar power that pollutes mother nature. Solar power doesn't release any greenhouse gasses, and except for needing a source of clean water to function, it uses absolutely no other resources. Hence, it's safe and environmentally-friendly.

What is the importance of energy statistics?

Annual energy statistics provide the most comprehensive coverage. On the supply side, data cover production, trade and stock changes. The transformation of energies is also included (for example electricity generation from natural gas and refining crude oil into motor gasoline).
