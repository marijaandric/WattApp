Bulb

Replace ordinary bulbs with LED bulbs.

Fridge

Set the temperature of the fridge to 3 - 5 degrees, and the freezer to -18 degrees.

Windows

Check that doors or windows are properly sealed.

Lighting

Turn off the lights in rooms where no one is.

Boiler

Set the boiler thermostat to 50-60 degrees.

TV

Turn off the TV and computer when you are not using them.

Machines

Turn on the washing machines when they are full, for a short time and at a low temperature.

Cooker

Align the pots and pans, turn off the oven 10 minutes before the end of cooking.

Efficiency

The most energy-efficient appliances and devices are marked with "A", "B" and "C".
